Cruddy Draw

About

This is a relatively small learning project for the seminar basics of digital communication in the sommer semester 2024 at the university of art Braunschweig.

How to use

You can draw on the grid by clicking and dragging your mouse. Hit s to save, hit n for new, hit d for delete.

s will save it to our KV store on val.town. You share your artwork with this link
